Ping Pong Score Tracker Project

Ping Pong score tracker keeps track of your score while you play.

Share your url with your opponent/friend and you can both update your scores in real time!

Feel like displaying your score on a big screen? Then share the special projector screen and let others watch your match scores in real time!

Features
  • Real time updates
  • Customize names(try emojis) and colors
  • Results Screens
  • Scores are announced using Web Speech API (can be turned off from settings
  • Projector screen to share your scores on the big screen
  • Works on IE 10+, Safari, Chrome, Firefox, IOS and Android

Developed using aurelia framework, and materialize css for the front end and Firebase for the backend
